Pine Mount names its ICSE toppers
(Report in The Shillong Times on 11th Jul 2020)

SHILLONG: With the declaration of ICSE results, Pine Mount School on Friday released the names of the students who topped from the institution during the ISC 2020 examinations. According to the institution, from the Humanities department, Sayantika Paul scored the highest with 95.25 per cent, while Eshita Deb and YuktaChettri, who held the second and third position, scored 93.75 per cent and 92.5 per cent respectively. Similarly, from the Science department, Nanetta FN Massar scored the highest marks with 93 per cent while the second position was secured by SuhanaFiza L Nongrum and Anusuya Deb with 90.5 each per cent each. AnishaNongrum secured the third position with 90.25 per cent. From the Commerce department of the institution, Ibiangmidaka M Kharbithai, who got the highest marks from the department, scored 68.75 per cent while BaiasuklangWankhar and Cheryl Alexis Wannian, who secured the second and third position, scored 64.25 and 62.20 per cent respectively.
